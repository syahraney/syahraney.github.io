About Me

My name is Robbibah Syahrani, i am an undergraduate State University of Surabaya majoring in Visual Communication Design. I am really passionate to develop my idea and knowledge through graphic design. I have interest in layouting design, illustration and simple motion. As a Visual Communication Design student, I am constantly exploring and experimenting with different design techniques and software. I enjoy brainstorming creative ideas and bringing them to life through graphic design. One of my favorite aspects of design is layouting, where I get to play with composition and typography to create visually appealing designs. Overall, I am excited to continue learning and growing as a Visual Communication Design student, and look forward to exploring more areas of design in the future.
